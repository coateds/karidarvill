Other Lookouts

    Long ago I decided I was going to visit all the lookouts or former lookout sites in Washington - until I found out there were more   
    than 600 of them!  As of summer of 2017 I had managed to make it to 84 of them and 29 with structures.  My partner was with me
    for at least 10 of these over the past two years.  I have also visited some in Oregon and Wyoming.  I love my lookouts and hope
    others do also and will work to preserve them through stewardship programs.
