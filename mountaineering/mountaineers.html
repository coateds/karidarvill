Mountaineers

I have been with the Mountaineers off and on for over 15 years.  For most of that time, I didn't organize outings as meetup and family were keeping me busy.  I went to the lodges and some classes, but that was it.  My partner was quite active with the Mountaineers so I began leading outings with them in 2017.  I am a hike and backpack leader.  Member feedback on my outings has been highly positive.  I am working on my Super Volunteer badge this year.
